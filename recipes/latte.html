"Cafe Latte"

Italian epresso based coffee beverage

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/3 cup dark roast espresso

Steps

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan set over medium-low heat. Whisk briskly with a wire whisk to create foam
  2. Brew espresso and pour into four cups. Pour in milk, holding the foam back with a spoon. Spoon foam over the top.
